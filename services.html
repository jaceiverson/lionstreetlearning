FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see yours here, reach out — we're always happy to talk.

Early intervention is always beneficial. If you have any concerns about your child's speech or language development — at any age — it's worth having a conversation. We work with children as young as 18 months. Research consistently shows that the earlier support begins, the better the outcomes. Trust your instincts as a parent: if something feels off, reach out.

Every child is different, and progress timelines depend on the nature and severity of the need, as well as how consistently we work together. Many families notice meaningful improvements within 6–12 weeks. We set clear, measurable goals from the start and review progress regularly so you always know where your child stands.

We are currently a private-pay practice. We provide detailed superbills (itemized receipts) that you can submit to your insurance provider for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Many families receive partial reimbursement this way.

We offer both in-person and virtual (teletherapy) sessions to fit your family's needs and schedule. Research shows that teletherapy can be just as effective as in-person services for many areas of speech and language support. We'll work with you to determine the best format for your child during the initial consultation.

Sessions are designed to feel engaging and fun for your child — we use play-based learning, games, storytelling, and hands-on activities that target specific goals. A typical session is 30–45 minutes and includes a warm-up, focused skill-building activities, and a brief debrief with the parent at the end. Children often say they don't even realize they're "working"!

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Life happens — we understand! Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee to ensure our time can be offered to other families on our waitlist. Full details are provided in your intake paperwork.
